Breakout!

How to Play

Use the arrow keys to move the paddle and bounce the ball to break the bricks. Don't let the ball fall off the screen!

Overtime, the ball will also speed up, making it harder to catch!

If you run out of lives, the game will end and you will lose

Good Luck!
