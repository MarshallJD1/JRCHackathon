Use the mouse to move the paddle and bounce the ball to break the bricks. Don't let the ball fall off the screen!

The purple bricks are power-ups!

If you run out of lives, the game will end and you will be able to input your highscores!

On touch devices, use your finger to move the paddle.

Overtime, the ball will also speed up, making it harder to catch!
